UNION PUBLIC SERVICE COMMISSION

The current Chairman of the Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) is: Dr. Manoj Soni.

About Me

The Commission has between 9 to 11 members, including the Chairman.

The President of India appoints the UPSC Chairman and other members.

Each member holds office for a term of six years or until they reach the age of 65, whichever comes first.

.To recruit candidates for various civil services of the Government of India,
including the Indian Administrative Service (IAS), Indian1 Police Service (IPS), and Indian Foreign Service2 (IFS).

The examination consists of three stages:
Preliminary Examination (Prelims):
.Objective-type questions. .Two papers: General Studies Paper I and Civil Services Aptitude Test (CSAT) Paper II. .CSAT is a qualifying paper.
.Main Examination (Mains):
.Written examination with descriptive-type papers. .Nine papers, including essay, general studies, and optional subjects.
.Personality Test (Interview):
.An interview to assess the candidate's personality and suitability for civil services.
